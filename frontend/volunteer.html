Why Volunteer With Us?

At Lovelines International Christian Fellowship, volunteering is more than service — it is a divine opportunity to impact lives, restore dignity, and express the love of Christ in action.

We care for orphans, widows, the less privileged, the traumatized, and the oppressed. As a volunteer, you become a vessel of healing, hope, and transformation in communities across the world.

Areas We Need Volunteers

Humanitarian Outreach

Participate in food distribution, clothing drives, and relief programs for vulnerable communities.

Counselling Support

Assist in emotional support services, follow-ups, and coordination of counselling programs.

Prison & Hospital Visits

Join outreach teams to provide encouragement, prayer, and support to those in need.

Education & Mentorship

Support students, mentor youths, and promote discipline and academic excellence.

Media & Tech

Help with social media, website management, graphics, and digital outreach programs.

Evangelism & Missions

Be part of soul-winning missions, crusades, and spiritual outreach activities.
