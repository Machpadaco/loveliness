About Lovelines

Lovelines International Christian Fellowship is a global faith-based humanitarian and counseling organization dedicated to empowering the vulnerable, restoring the traumatized, supporting leaders and educators, and partnering with governments to promote peace, dignity, and societal stability.

We care for orphans, widows, prisoners, the less privileged, victims of abuse, and individuals facing emotional and spiritual trauma. Through counseling, humanitarian outreach, empowerment programs, and advocacy, we restore dignity, hope, and purpose.

Our Mission Focus

Caring for the vulnerable, restoring hope, building lives, and transforming communities through love in action.

Our Key Services

Humanitarian Relief

Food distribution, clothing support, emergency relief outreach programs and welfare support for vulnerable communities and displaced persons.

Counseling & Healing

Professional faith-centered counseling including trauma recovery, marriage counseling, youth mentoring and emotional healing.

Care for the Vulnerable

Dedicated support for widows, orphans, prisoners, elderly people and individuals living with disabilities.

Community Development

Skill acquisition, youth and women empowerment, educational support, health awareness and poverty alleviation initiatives.

Advocacy & Awareness

Campaigns against abuse, domestic violence, drug abuse, and community peace-building initiatives.

Faith & Spiritual Growth

Biblical counseling, prayer support, leadership development and evangelism missions across communities.
