Who We Are

Lovelines International Christian Fellowship is a faith-based humanitarian and counseling organization committed to caring for, restoring, and empowering vulnerable individuals and communities across the world.

We are deeply involved in supporting orphans, motherless children, the less privileged, the traumatized, the oppressed, victims of rape and abuse, prisoners, and other vulnerable groups. Through compassion-driven interventions, counseling, empowerment programs, and advocacy, we work to restore dignity, hope, and purpose to lives affected by hardship, injustice, and trauma.

Healing & Counseling

We provide professional and faith-centered counseling services, focusing on emotional healing, trauma recovery, and psychosocial support. We believe healing the mind and soul is essential to building stable societies.

Capacity Building

We equip church leaders, pastors, and missionaries with guidance, leadership wisdom, and spiritual clarity for effective service in the work of Christ.

Education Support

We offer advisory services to schools, helping proprietors, teachers, and students promote moral values, discipline, and academic excellence.

Our Global Impact

With a presence in nearly **two hundred countries**, our impact-driven approach has earned recognition and collaboration with government authorities to help restore order, promote peace, and bring stability to society.
